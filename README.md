# BiteLog - Social Nutrition Tracker

## Problem Statement

We’re building a social nutrition tracking app where users can:

- Upload food photos
- Automatically detect calories and macros using AI
- Follow friends or celebrities to view their meals and progress
- Compete on goals like calorie targets or protein intake

It’s like Instagram meets MyFitnessPal, with a collaborative and competitive twist.

---

## What’s The Problem?

Many people struggle to maintain healthy eating habits due to:

- Lack of awareness
- Lack of accountability
- Lack of motivation

Existing calorie-counting apps are often isolated experiences that:

- Don’t encourage consistency
- Lack social support

This leads to users abandoning their health goals or inaccurately tracking nutrition.

---

## Why Is It Important?

- Over **40.3%** of U.S. adults are overweight or obese, increasing risks for chronic diseases such as diabetes and heart disease (CDC).
- A 2021 NIH study shows **social accountability significantly improves diet adherence**.
- Most nutrition apps lack community or social features.
- People are more likely to stick to fitness and diet routines with social support, friendly competition, and public progress tracking.

---

## How Our Project Solves The Problem

Our app addresses motivation and consistency by combining:

- AI-powered calorie and macro tracking
- A social media-style platform

Users can:

- Upload food photos and instantly get nutritional breakdowns
- Share meals with friends

This turns a tedious, solo task into a fun and interactive experience.

By introducing accountability through social connection, users stay engaged and on track with their health goals.

---

## What Makes Our Solution Unique

Unlike existing calorie counter apps, our platform is social at its core:

- Users can follow friends or fitness influencers
- View meals and compete in weekly macro-based challenges
- Gamified progress tracking with a shared feed

We streamline meal logging using:

- Image recognition APIs
- Nutrition APIs

No manual typing or barcode scanning needed.

This unique blend transforms calorie counting into a shared, motivating experience.
